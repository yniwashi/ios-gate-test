CHECKLIST 2 : Per RSI - 60 SECONDS COUNTDOWN
Nasal Cannula @ 15l/min 1 5 Commence progressive laryngoscopy & intubation
Ensure pharyngeal patency 2 6 Call out O₂ Sats, HR, Pulse Character, MAP, ECG
Acknowledge ETCO₂ change & Apnea Alarm 3 7 Cycle NIBP after 60 secs , call out SBP, DP & MAP
Connect Inline ETCO₂ once apnea is confirmed 4 8 ETT placement: Direct visual, ETCO₂ waves X 5, A/E

Medication Dosing : Adjust doses according to hemodynamic stability, Shock Index, Age & Co-morbidities
